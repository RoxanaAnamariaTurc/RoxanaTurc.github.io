Following steps have been considered in order to deliver the website

"Roxie's gym"

Objectives/Goals

The website's goals are to encourage women of all ages to
stay active and embrace the weights lifting training.

Our main objectives when creating the website is to ensure women follow a healthy diet
that is easy to maintain.

Key audience

The audience of the website will be women of all ages that are starting their
fitness journey and are looking for programs and easy recipes
to fit within their busy lifes.

Provisional site structure

The website will have the following pages for the
users to interact with:


  • Welcome Page
  • About us Page
  • Training exercises videos and photos page
  • Recipes and Food guidance page
  • A feedback page
  • A contact us page
  • A shopping page

    • Site map

    Wireframes




    Functional specification

    The website will have clear links to move between pages;

    A reset button that will allow users to automatically
    delete all the fields completed on the feedback page and contact us page;

    The website will be available to open on various browsers such as Google Chrome,
    Microsoft Edge, Firefox.
    There will also be a mobile version of the website.

    Competitors analysis

    In order to analyze our competitors we have created feedback forms for users to complete and submit;

    The website will have personas created in order to trial the functionalities;

    Competitors fitness apps and websites have been checked and studied in order to
    deliver a better user experience.
